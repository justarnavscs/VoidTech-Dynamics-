Zen Launcher Help

Your guide to using the Zen Launcher effectively

About Zen Launcher

Zen Launcher is a customizable web application launcher designed to help you organize and access your favorite web apps and tools from a single, clean interface. With its minimalist design and intuitive functionality, Zen Launcher enhances your productivity while maintaining a peaceful user experience.

Key Features

Add Apps

Easily add applications to your home screen using the plus button. You can choose from a wide selection of pre-configured apps or add your own custom web links.

Edit Mode

Enter edit mode to remove apps from your home screen. Simply click the edit button, then click the remove (X) button on any app you wish to delete (except the Help app).

Search Functionality

Quickly find apps by typing in the search bar. The launcher will filter your apps in real-time as you type.

Time & Date

Always stay informed with the current time and date displayed prominently at the top of the launcher.

How to Use Zen Launcher

Adding Apps to Home Screen

  1. Click the green plus button at the bottom right of the screen
  2. Browse through the available apps in the modal window
  3. Click on any app to add it to your home screen
  4. The app will now appear on your main screen for easy access

Removing Apps from Home Screen

  1. Click the blue edit button (pencil icon) to enter edit mode
  2. Click the red remove (X) button on any app you wish to delete
  3. The app will be immediately removed from your home screen
  4. Click the edit button again to exit edit mode

Searching for Apps

  1. Click on the search bar at the top of the screen
  2. Start typing the name of the app you're looking for
  3. Apps will be filtered in real-time as you type
  4. Clear the search bar to see all apps again

Tips & Tricks

  • Landscape Mode: Zen Launcher automatically adjusts to landscape orientation by hiding the header and footer for more app space.
  • App Organization: Add your most frequently used apps for quick access.
  • Help Always Available: The Help app cannot be removed, so you can always access this guide.
  • Data Persistence: Your app selections are saved locally, so they'll be there when you return.
